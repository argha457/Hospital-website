Cardio Monitoring

Our hospital provides state-of-the-art cardiac monitoring services for patients with heart conditions. From continuous ECG monitoring to Holter monitoring, we offer comprehensive cardiac care to ensure your heart health. Contact our cardiology department to schedule an appointment or learn more about our services.

Medical Treatment

Receive expert medical treatment and care at Argha Hospital. Our experienced team of healthcare professionals is dedicated to providing personalized treatment plans tailored to your needs. Whether you require surgery, medication management, or rehabilitation services, we're here to help you on your journey to recovery. Contact us today to schedule an appointment or learn more about our medical services.

Emergency Help

In case of medical emergencies, our hospital is here to provide immediate assistance. Call 911/369 for life-threatening emergencies or visit our emergency department for prompt medical care. Our team of emergency healthcare providers is available 24/7 to address your urgent medical needs. Your health and safety are our top priorities.

First Aid

For minor injuries or medical emergencies, administer basic first aid as needed. Seek professional medical help for severe injuries or conditions. Visit our emergency department for immediate assistance or call 911/369 for life-threatening emergencies.
